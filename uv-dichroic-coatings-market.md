<p><p>UV Dichroic Coatings are used in various applications, including optics, architectural glass, and artwork. These coatings are classified into two main types - Translucent and Opaque. Translucent type coatings allow the transmission of some light while reflecting or absorbing certain wavelengths, giving a colored appearance. They are commonly used in decorative glass and lighting applications. On the other hand, Opaque type coatings completely block the transmission of light, providing a nontransparent or reflective surface. These coatings are often used in mirrors and privacy glass applications.</p></p>
