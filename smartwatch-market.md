<p><p>The smartwatch market can be categorized into three types: Companion Smartwatch, Standalone Smartwatch, and Classic Smartwatch. A Companion Smartwatch requires connectivity with a smartphone to fully function and relies on the phone for most features. A Standalone Smartwatch, on the other hand, operates independently without the need for a companion smartphone. It can make calls, send messages, and access internet services on its own. Lastly, Classic Smartwatches are designed to resemble traditional timepieces, focusing on aesthetics while offering limited smart features.</p></p>
