<p><p>Vinyl Composition Tile (VCT) is a type of flooring material commonly used in commercial and residential spaces. It is available in two market types: single layer and multilayer. Single layer VCT is made using a single layer of vinyl composition, and it provides basic durability and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, multilayer VCT consists of multiple layers, including a clear wear layer that enhances durability and performance. Multilayer VCT is usually more expensive but offers superior protection against stains, scratches, and wear and tear.</p></p>
