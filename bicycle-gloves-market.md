<p><p>Bicycle gloves are categorized into two main types: half-finger and full-finger gloves. Half-finger bicycle gloves provide palm protection and a better grip while leaving the fingers exposed for increased dexterity and airflow. They are suitable for warmer climates or riders who prefer the freedom of bare fingers. In contrast, full-finger bicycle gloves offer full coverage with protection for the palms, knuckles, and fingers. These gloves are ideal for colder weather, off-road biking, or riders seeking extra hand protection. Both types cater to different preferences and conditions within the bicycle gloves market.</p></p>
