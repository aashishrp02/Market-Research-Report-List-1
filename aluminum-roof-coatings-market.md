<p><ul><li>Fiber</li><li>Non-Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aluminum roof coatings are classified into two main types: fiber and non-fiber. Fiber aluminum roof coatings contain fibers, such as polyester or fiberglass, which enhance the coating's strength and durability. These coatings are highly resistant to cracks and tears, making them ideal for areas with harsh weather conditions. On the other hand, non-fiber aluminum roof coatings do not contain any reinforcing fibers. They are generally more affordable and easier to apply, but may not offer the same level of resilience as fiber coatings. Both types provide protection and reflectivity, extending a roof's lifespan and reducing energy consumption.</p></p>
