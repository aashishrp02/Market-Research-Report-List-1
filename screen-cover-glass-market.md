<p><p>Screen cover glass is a protective layer that is placed on top of the display of electronic devices such as smartphones, tablets, and laptops. The growing demand for smartphones and tablets, coupled with the rising trend of larger screen sizes, has created a need for more durable and reliable screen protection. <p><p>The screen cover glass market consists of three main types: synthetic sapphire, tempered glass, and PMMA. Synthetic sapphire is a highly durable and scratch-resistant material often used in high-end smartphones and watches. Tempered glass, on the other hand, is made by heating and cooling regular glass, making it stronger and more resistant to breakage. It is commonly used in various electronic devices. PMMA (polymethyl methacrylate) is a lightweight and impact-resistant plastic commonly used as a cheaper alternative to tempered glass in lower-end electronic devices.</p></p>
