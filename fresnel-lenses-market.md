<p><ul><li>Imaging Type</li><li>Non-imaging Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fresnel lenses market can be broadly classified into two types: imaging type and non-imaging type. Imaging type lenses are designed to concentrate light rays onto a specific focal point, commonly used in applications such as optics, photography, and projectors. Non-imaging type lenses, on the other hand, are used to distribute light rays evenly, typically employed in applications like solar energy, LED lighting, and navigation systems. While imaging lenses focus light, non-imaging lenses disperse or redirect light for specific purposes in various industries and technological applications.</p></p>
