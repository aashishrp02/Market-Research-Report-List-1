Firstly, the increasing focus on security and safety measures in buildings is creating a greater demand for door restrictors. These devices provide an added layer of security by limiting the door's range of motion, making it more difficult for unwanted individuals to gain access.</p><p>Additionally, the rising awareness about energy efficiency is another factor contributing to market growth. Door restrictors can prevent doors from swinging open too wide, reducing air leakage and preventing energy loss. As energy efficiency regulations become more stringent, the demand for door restrictors is expected to increase further.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing construction industry, particularly in emerging economies, is driving the demand for door restrictors.
