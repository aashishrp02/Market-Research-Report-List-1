<p><p>Cold-pressed sesame oil is segmented into two main market types: organic and conventional. Organic cold-pressed sesame oil is derived from sesame seeds that are grown without the use of synthetic pesticides, fertilizers, or genetically modified organisms (GMOs). It is produced in accordance with organic farming practices and is certified by authorized entities. On the other hand, conventional cold-pressed sesame oil is made from sesame seeds that may be conventionally cultivated using synthetic pesticides and fertilizers. It does not carry any organic certification. The choice between organic and conventional sesame oil depends on consumers' preferences for organic products and their concern for the use of synthetic chemicals.</p></p>
