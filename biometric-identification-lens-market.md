<p><p>The Biometric Identification Lens market includes three main types: Optical Zoom, Digital Zoom, and Other. Optical Zoom refers to the lens mechanism that physically adjusts the focal length, allowing users to zoom in and out without losing image quality. Digital Zoom, on the other hand, enlarges the image by digitally cropping and enlarging the pixels, resulting in a lower-quality image. The "Other" market within this industry comprises different lenses, such as fixed lenses or specialty lenses, designed for specific applications or requirements.</p></p>
