<p><p>Residential epoxy floor coatings are classified into three types: water-based, solvent-based, and 100% solids. Water-based coatings are made by diluting epoxy resins with water, which makes them easy to apply and environmentally friendly. Solvent-based coatings are made by dissolving epoxy resins in solvents, providing a durable and high-gloss finish. 100% solids coatings are made without any solvents or water, resulting in a thicker application and superior durability. Each type has its unique characteristics and benefits, catering to different preferences and requirements in residential applications.</p></p>
