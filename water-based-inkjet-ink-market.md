<p><p>Water-based inkjet ink market consists of two major types: pigment ink and dye ink. Pigment ink is made up of tiny solid particles that are suspended in water, providing vibrant and long-lasting colors that are resistant to fading. This type of ink is commonly used for printing documents and photos that require high durability. On the other hand, dye ink is composed of liquid colorants that dissolve in water, delivering vivid and vibrant colors with excellent color saturation. Dye ink is often used for printing documents and photos that prioritize color vibrancy over long-term durability.</p></p>
