<p><p>The leatherette market primarily consists of three types: PVC (Poly Vinyl Chloride), PU (Polyurethane), and Bio-Based materials. PVC leatherette is made from a synthetic polymer called vinyl chloride, which offers excellent durability and water resistance. PU leatherette, on the other hand, is produced using a polymer known as polyurethane, providing a soft and supple feel similar to genuine leather. Bio-Based leatherette materials are derived from natural sources and are more environmentally friendly. They are made using plant-based polymers, reducing the carbon footprint in their production process.</p></p>
