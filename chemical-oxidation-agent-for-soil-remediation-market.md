<p><p>Chemical oxidation agents, used in soil remediation, are substances that promote oxidation reactions to break down harmful contaminants into less toxic forms. Different types of chemical oxidation agents include permanganate, sodium persulfate, hydrogen peroxide, zero-valent iron, and others. Permanganate is effective for treating a wide range of contaminants, while sodium persulfate is particularly useful for organic compounds. Hydrogen peroxide works well in treating volatile organic compounds, while zero-valent iron is effective for heavy metals and chlorinated solvents. Other types of chemical oxidation agents may include ozone, chlorine, and peroxides. Each agent has its own specific applications and benefits in soil remediation.</p></p>
