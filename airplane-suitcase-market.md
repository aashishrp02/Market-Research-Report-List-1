<p><p>The airplane suitcase market can be divided into two major types: soft suitcases and hard suitcases. Soft suitcases are made of flexible materials such as nylon or polyester and offer lighter weight and better flexibility, making them ideal for fitting into tight spaces on an airplane. On the other hand, hard suitcases are constructed using rigid materials like polycarbonate or ABS, providing greater protection for fragile items. They are generally more durable and resistant to impact, making them suitable for travelers who prioritize security and durability over flexibility.</p></p>
