The MOV market can be categorized into two types: Low Voltage (LV) MOV and High Voltage-Medium Voltage (HV-MV) MOV. LV MOVs are used in low voltage applications, such as residential and small commercial settings, where electrical input is typically below 600 volts. On the other hand, HV-MV MOVs are employed in high voltage applications, like industrial and utility settings, where electrical input exceeds 600 volts.</p></p>
