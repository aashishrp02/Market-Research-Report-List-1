<p><p>Hair colorants market types can be categorized as temporary, semi-permanent, and permanent. Temporary hair colorants provide a short-term solution as they easily wash out after a few shampoo sessions. Semi-permanent colorants offer a longer-lasting effect, fading naturally after several weeks. Permanent hair colorants penetrate the hair shaft, providing a lasting color change until new hair growth occurs.
