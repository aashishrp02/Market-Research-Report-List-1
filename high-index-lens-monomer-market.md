<p><p>The high index lens monomer market includes three types: 1.60 index, 1.67 index, and 1.70 index. These types represent different levels of lens thickness and refraction capabilities. The 1.60 index lenses are thinner than regular lenses and provide better vision correction for people with moderate to high prescriptions. The 1.67 index lenses are even thinner and suitable for those with higher prescriptions. Lastly, the 1.70 index lenses are the thinnest and ideal for individuals with very high prescriptions, providing maximum vision correction.</p></p>
