<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Electrical electronics adhesives market is divided into two major types: thermally conductive adhesives and conductive adhesives. </p><p>Thermally conductive adhesives are designed to facilitate the transfer of heat between electrical components and heat sinks, ensuring proper functionality and preventing overheating. These adhesives are commonly used in electronic devices such as computers, LED lighting, and power modules.</p><p>Conductive adhesives, on the other hand, are used to establish electrical connections between components. They contain conductive fillers that allow the flow of electricity, enabling the bonding of electronic circuitry. This type of adhesive is commonly used in applications like circuit boards, electrical connectors, and sensors.</p></p>
