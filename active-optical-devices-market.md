Photodetectors are used to convert the optical signal back into an electrical form for further processing. Optical amplifiers are utilized to boost the strength of the optical signal, enabling it to travel longer distances without degradation. Lastly, there are other active optical devices that serve various functions like wavelength division multiplexers, optical switches, and optical attenuators.</p></p>
