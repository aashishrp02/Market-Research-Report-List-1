<p><p>The wire-pulling lubricant market offers different types of lubricants: liquid, wax, and gel. Liquid lubricants are fluid in nature, allowing smooth application. Wax lubricants are solid at room temperature but melt easily when heated, providing a thick coating. Gel lubricants have a semi-solid consistency, offering excellent adherence while staying in place during wire pulling. These lubricants are designed to minimize friction, ease cable pulling, and reduce the chances of damage to wires. Each type has its advantages and characteristics, catering to various preferences and requirements in the wire-pulling industry.</p></p>
