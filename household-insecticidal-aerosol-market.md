<p><p>The household insecticidal aerosol market includes two types of aerosols: foam aerosols and powder aerosols. Foam aerosols are aerosol products that dispense as foam when sprayed, usually used for spraying on surfaces such as walls, floors, or furniture to control insect infestations. Powder aerosols, on the other hand, dispense as a fine powder when sprayed and are typically used to control insects in hard-to-reach areas or cracks and crevices. Both types of aerosols are commonly used in households as an effective way to eliminate and prevent insect problems.</p></p>
