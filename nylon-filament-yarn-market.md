<p><p>The Nylon Filament Yarn market is divided into four types: Flat, POY, FDY, and HSO. Flat yarn is a thin and smooth variant used in the production of high-quality fabrics. POY (Partially Oriented Yarn) is partially stretched and has a higher elasticity, making it suitable for applications like sportswear and hosiery. FDY (Fully Drawn Yarn) is fully stretched and has excellent strength and durability, commonly used in the production of textiles like carpets and curtains. HSO (High-Strength Yarn) is a stronger variant primarily used in industrial applications and technical textiles.</p></p>
