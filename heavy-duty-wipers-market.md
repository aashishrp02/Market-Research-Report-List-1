<p><p>The heavy-duty wipers market offers two types of products: dry wipers and pre-saturated wipers. Dry wipers are designed to be used with a separate cleaning solution or solvent. They are effective in removing dirt, grease, and other contaminants from surfaces. Both types of wipers are widely used in industries that require heavy-duty cleaning, such as automotive, manufacturing, and healthcare.</p></p>
