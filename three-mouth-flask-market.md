<p><p>Three mouth flasks are a type of laboratory flask used for holding and mixing liquids. The straight three mouth flask has three openings which are aligned horizontally, allowing for easy pouring and transferring of liquids. Both flasks cater to different laboratory requirements, with the inclined design offering more precision.</p></p>
