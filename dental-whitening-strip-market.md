<p><p>The dental whitening strip market includes two main types: gel whitestrips and dry whitestrips. Gel whitestrips are thin, flexible strips that are coated with a whitening gel containing hydrogen peroxide or carbamide peroxide. They are applied directly to the teeth and left on for a specific period of time. Dry whitestrips, on the other hand, are strips that are pre-coated with a dry whitening formula and do not require any additional gels or liquids. Users simply apply the strips to their teeth and leave them on for the designated time. Both types aim to whiten teeth and improve their appearance.</p></p>
