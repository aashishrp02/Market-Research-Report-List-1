<p><p>Virgin polyester staple fiber is a type of synthetic fiber commonly used in the textile industry. It can be categorized into two market types: solid fiber and hollow fiber. Solid fiber refers to polyester staples with a consistent diameter throughout their length, providing a uniform and dense structure. On the other hand, hollow fiber has a hollow center, which enhances its insulation properties and makes it suitable for applications requiring warmth or lightweight fabrics. Both solid and hollow fiber have their unique advantages and are utilized in various textile products according to specific requirements.</p></p>
