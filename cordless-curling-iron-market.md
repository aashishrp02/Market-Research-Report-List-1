<p><p>The cordless curling iron market can be divided into two types: automatic and manual. Automatic cordless curling irons are designed with built-in timers and rotating barrels to create effortless curls. They allow users to set the desired temperature and time, and the iron does the rest. On the other hand, manual cordless curling irons require users to manually wrap their hair around the barrel and hold it in place for a certain amount of time to achieve the desired curl. Both types provide convenience and mobility, making hairstyling on-the-go easier.</p></p>
