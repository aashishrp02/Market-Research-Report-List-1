<p><p>The electric transformer market is segmented into various types based on their functionality and design. Isolation transformers are used to provide electrical isolation between the input and output, ensuring safety and protection against electric shock. Autotransformers are designed for voltage regulation and have a single winding with taps to adjust the output voltage. Toroidal transformers offer high efficiency and low electromagnetic interference due to their toroidal shape. Other types include step-up and step-down transformers, power transformers, and distribution transformers, catering to different applications and power capacities. These transformers are crucial in electrical distribution and transmission systems for efficient power transfer.</p></p>
