<p><p>The smart sewing machine market consists of two main types: single head and multi-head machines. Single head machines are designed for individual use, with only one sewing head that can perform different stitching tasks. They are compact and suitable for small-scale production or personal sewing projects. On the other hand, multi-head machines have multiple sewing heads that can work simultaneously, enabling faster and more efficient production in larger-scale operations. These machines are commonly used in textile factories or garment production facilities.</p></p>
