<p><strong>The Steel Abrasive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Steel Shot</li><li>Steel Grit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel abrasive market comprises two main types: steel shot and steel grit. Steel shot is produced by melting scrap metal and forming spherical pellets through a process known as atomization. It is widely used in applications that require a smooth finish or peening effect, such as cleaning, deburring, or shot peening. On the other hand, steel grit is made by crushing steel shots into angular particles. It is commonly used for surface preparation tasks that require aggressive cleaning, such as removing rust, scaling, or old paint.</p></p>
