Secondly, the forged steel camshaft is created through a shaping process using heated steel, providing durability and strength for high-performance engines. Lastly, the assembled camshaft is composed of individual cam lobes that are assembled onto a common shaft, allowing for customization and flexibility based on specific engine requirements.</p></p>
