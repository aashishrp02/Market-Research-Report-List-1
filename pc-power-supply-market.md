<p><p>The PC power supply market consists of various types, including ATX, SFX, and others. ATX (Advanced Technology eXtended) power supplies are the most common type, designed for standard desktop PCs. SFX (Small Form Factor) power supplies are compact and suited for smaller cases, ideal for mini-ITX or micro-ATX builds. Other market options include TFX (Thin Form Factor), which are even smaller and used in slim desktop models, and specialized form factors like Flex ATX or AT. Each type caters to different PC sizes and form factors, providing power solutions specific to varying computer requirements.</p></p>
