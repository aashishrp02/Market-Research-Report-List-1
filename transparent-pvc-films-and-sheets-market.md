<p><p>Transparent PVC films and sheets are widely used in various industries for packaging, printing, and protection purposes. They are manufactured using three different technologies - Calendering, Extrusion, and Laminating. Calendering technology involves passing softened PVC through a series of rollers to produce thin and smooth films. Extrusion technology utilizes a process of melting PVC chips and extruding them through a die to form films or sheets. Laminating technology involves bonding multiple layers of PVC films or sheets together to enhance the strength and functionality of the final product.</p></p>
