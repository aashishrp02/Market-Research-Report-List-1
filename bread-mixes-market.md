Firstly, the rising demand for convenience food products and the growing trend of home baking are driving the market growth. Bread mixes offer a simple and time-saving solution for consumers who want to enjoy freshly baked bread at home without the hassle of measuring and mixing ingredients from scratch.</p><p>Furthermore, the increasing awareness of the nutritional benefits associated with homemade bread is also contributing to the market growth. Consumers are becoming more health-conscious and are looking for healthier alternatives to store-bought bread, which often contains preservatives and artificial ingredients. Bread mixes enable consumers to control the ingredients and tailor the bread to their specific dietary needs.</p><p>Moreover, the COVID-19 pandemic has further fueled the market growth.
