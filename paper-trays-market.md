<p><p>The paper trays market comprises three main types: corrugated board, boxboard/carton, and molded pulp. Corrugated board trays are made from sturdy, layered cardboard and are commonly used for shipping and storing products. Boxboard/carton trays are crafted from thicker paperboard and are widely utilized in packaging applications for products like food and beverages. Finally, molded pulp trays are formed by pressing recycled paper fibers into a specific shape and are frequently employed for product protection and display purposes. Each type of tray offers unique features and benefits to meet varying industry needs.</p></p>
