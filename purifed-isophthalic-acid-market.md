<p><p>Purified Isophthalic Acid (PIA) is a white crystalline powder that is used primarily as a raw material in the production of unsaturated polyester resins (UPR). PIA is manufactured by oxidizing meta-xylene and is purified through a multi-step process to remove impurities and ensure high quality. It exhibits excellent chemical resistance, heat resistance, and dimensional stability, making it suitable for various applications such as automotive, construction, electrical, and marine industries.</p><p>The Purified Isophthalic Acid Market is expected to witness substantial growth during the forecast period. The increasing demand for polyester resins in various end-use industries is the key driver for market growth. Rising construction activities, infrastructural development, and growing automotive production are driving the demand for PIA. Additionally, the growing demand for lightweight materials in the automotive sector and the expanding electrical and electronics industry are further fueling market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the increasing use of PIA in the manufacturing of fiberglass reinforced plastics (FRP) is boosting market growth. FRP finds applications in construction, automotive, and electrical industries due to its high strength, durability, and resistance to corrosion. The growing adoption of FRP composites in the construction of wind turbine blades, pipes, tanks, and structural components is generating significant demand for PIA.</p><p>Moreover, the rising focus on sustainable and eco-friendly materials is also driving the demand for PIA. The development of bio-based isophthalic acid that offers reduced environmental impact is expected to further augment market growth.</p><p>In conclusion, the Purifed Isophthalic Acid Market is predicted to witness impressive growth owing to the increasing demand for polyester resins, expanding end-use industries, and the shift towards sustainable materials.
