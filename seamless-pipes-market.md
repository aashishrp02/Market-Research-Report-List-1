<p><p>Seamless pipes are used in various industries such as oil and gas, water transport, and construction. They are produced through two different processes: hot-rolling and cold-rolling. Hot-rolled seamless pipes are made by heating a solid billet and then rolling it into a tube shape. This process allows for better dimensional accuracy, improved surface finish, and increased mechanical properties. On the other hand, cold-rolled seamless pipes are made by passing a solid cylindrical billet through a series of rollers at room temperature. This process results in a smoother and more precise pipe with increased strength and flexibility.</p></p>
