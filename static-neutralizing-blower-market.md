<p><strong>The Static Neutralizing Blower Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The static neutralizing blower market is divided into active and passive types. Active static neutralizing blowers use electrical power to generate ions that neutralize static charges on objects. They are efficient in neutralizing charges on a wide range of materials. On the other hand, passive static neutralizing blowers use natural airflow to neutralize static charges. They do not require electrical power but are less effective in neutralizing charges on non-conductive materials. Both types are used in industries where static electricity can cause issues, such as electronics manufacturing and printing.</p></p>
