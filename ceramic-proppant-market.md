The increasing demand for oil and gas, coupled with advancements in drilling technologies, is expected to drive the market growth. Compared to other proppants like sand and resin-coated sand, ceramic proppants have better conductivity and are more resistant to crushing, resulting in improved well productivity.</p><p>Moreover, the market is benefiting from ongoing research and development activities, leading to the development of innovative ceramic proppant products. Manufacturers are investing in the production of lightweight and low-density ceramic proppants, which offer improved performance and cost-efficiency.</p><p>However, the market may face challenges due to the increasing adoption of alternate proppants like resin-coated proppants and the fluctuating oil prices.
