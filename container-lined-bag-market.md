<p><ul><li>Single-Layer Flexitank</li><li>Double Layer Flexitank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The container lined bag market includes two main types: single-layer flexitanks and double-layer flexitanks. A single-layer flexitank is a type of container lined bag that consists of a single layer of material, typically made from high-strength polyethylene. It is used for transporting non-hazardous liquids in large quantities. On the other hand, a double-layer flexitank is similar in function but has an additional layer of protection, which enhances its durability and resistance to potential leaks or damages. Both types are cost-effective and efficient solutions for bulk liquid transportation.</p></p>
