<p><p>Spindle oils are lubricants used in machine tools to minimize friction and heat generated during high-speed operations. Type FC spindle oil is a general-purpose lubricant with high oxidation resistance, suitable for machines with moderate speeds and loads. On the other hand, type FD spindle oil is a specialized lubricant formulated for machines operating at high speeds and loads, providing enhanced protection against wear and heat buildup. Both types of spindle oils are designed to improve the performance and longevity of machine tools by reducing friction and optimizing lubrication.</p></p>
