<p><p>The geotextile tube market can be categorized into two types: woven and nonwoven. Woven geotextile tubes are made by weaving synthetic fibers together, providing them with high tensile strength and durability. On the other hand, nonwoven geotextile tubes are manufactured by bonding fibers together using heat or chemicals, giving them excellent filtration properties. Both types of geotextile tubes are widely used in applications such as coastal protection, dewatering, and shoreline stabilization, providing cost-effective and environmentally friendly solutions for various construction projects.</p></p>
