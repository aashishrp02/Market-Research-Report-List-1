<p><p>Integrated Circuits (IC) Process Chemicals Market Types refer to the different chemical components utilized in the production of integrated circuits. Dry film chemicals are used for securing the integrated circuits during the fabrication process. Inks are utilized for printing circuit patterns on substrates. ENIG (Electroless Nickel Immersion Gold) chemicals are employed for surface treatments to protect circuitry. Photoresist chemicals are used to selectively block or expose areas for circuit etching. Etchants are utilized to remove unwanted materials from circuit boards. Contrast medium chemicals enhance contrast for better circuit inspection. Other market types may include cleaning agents, solvents, and specialty chemicals for specific production requirements.</p></p>
