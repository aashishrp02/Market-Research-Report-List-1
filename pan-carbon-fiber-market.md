<p><p>The PAN Carbon Fiber market consists of two main types: regular-tow carbon fiber and large-tow carbon fiber. Regular-tow carbon fiber is made from smaller bundles of filaments, typically ranging from 3,000 to 24,000 individual filaments. It is commonly used in applications where high strength and stiffness are required, such as aerospace and sporting goods. On the other hand, large-tow carbon fiber is made from larger bundles of filaments, typically ranging from 48,000 to 320,000 individual filaments. It is often used in applications where cost-effectiveness and high production rates are prioritized, such as automotive and wind energy industries.</p></p>
