<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Airborne Molecular Contamination (AMC) Filters market includes two types of filters - Chemisorption Filters and Bonded Media Panels. Chemisorption Filters are made of either activated carbon or aluminas and are designed to chemically react with and remove molecular contaminants from the air. On the other hand, Bonded Media Panels consist of activated carbon formed into single-piece panels. These panels provide a larger surface area for capturing and adsorbing airborne contaminants. Both types of filters play a crucial role in maintaining clean air quality by effectively removing harmful molecules from the surrounding air.</p></p>
