<p><p>Flux coated preforms are pre-made soldering alloys that have a layer of flux coating applied to them. Flux coated preforms offer several advantages over traditional flux application methods, including ease of use, reduced waste, and improved quality control.</p><p>The flux coated preforms market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period. The market is driven by the increasing demand for electronics products, which require reliable soldering techniques. This trend is driven by the need for higher reliability and performance in electronic devices.</p><p>Another notable trend in the market is the increasing adoption of automation in the soldering process.
