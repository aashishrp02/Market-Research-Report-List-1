<p><p>The decorative films and foils market is categorized into various types based on the materials used. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride), PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate), PP (Polypropylene), and others are the key segments. PVC films offer high moisture resistance and durability, making them ideal for applications such as home decor, automotive interiors, and packaging. PET films are known for their excellent optical clarity, making them suitable for products like labels, displays, and laminates. PP films offer good chemical resistance and are commonly used in food packaging. The "Others" category includes films and foils made from materials like polystyrene, polyamide, and polyester.</p></p>
