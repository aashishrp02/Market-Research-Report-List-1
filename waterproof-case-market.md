<p><ul><li>Hard Shell</li><li>Soft Bag</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The waterproof case market consists of two main types: hard shell cases and soft bag cases. Hard shell cases are made of rigid materials, such as plastic or aluminum, providing a durable and protective enclosure for electronic devices or valuables. They often feature a secure sealing mechanism to prevent water ingress. On the other hand, soft bag cases are made of flexible and waterproof materials, such as PVC or nylon, offering a lightweight and versatile option. They commonly use roll-top closures or zippers to ensure water resistance.</p></p>
