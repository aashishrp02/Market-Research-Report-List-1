<p><p>The light vehicle steering market is divided into four types. Electric Powered Hydraulic Steering combines hydraulic assistance with an electric motor, offering improved efficiency and control. Electric Powered Steering Systems utilize an electric motor to assist steering, providing smooth and responsive handling. Hydraulic Powered Steering uses hydraulic pressure to aid in steering, ensuring ease of maneuverability. Manual Steering requires physical effort from the driver to steer the vehicle but is generally less expensive. Each type caters to different preferences and requirements in the light vehicle steering market.</p></p>
