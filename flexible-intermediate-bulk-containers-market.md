<p><p>Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers (FIBCs) are widely used in industries for storage and transportation of bulk materials. There are various types of FIBCs based on their safety features and conductivity. Type A FIBCs are non-conductive and should not be used for flammable materials. Type B FIBCs do not have any static protection. Type C FIBCs are conductive and protect against electrostatic discharge. Type D FIBCs are specifically designed with conducting threads and protect against hazardous static electricity, making them suitable for flammable materials.</p></p>
