There are two main types of reflective materials in the market: glass beads and microprismatic. Glass beads reflect light back to its source through a spherical shape, providing effective visibility. Microprismatic materials use special prisms to reflect light in multiple directions, resulting in increased visibility from various angles. Both options are popular in the clothing market for their ability to improve safety and visibility for workers, athletes, and outdoor enthusiasts.</p></p>
