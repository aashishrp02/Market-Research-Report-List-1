<p><p>The Camera Module & Touch Sensor Assembly Adhesives market offers two types of adhesives: UV Curable Type and Non-UV Curable Type. UV Curable adhesives utilize ultraviolet light to cure and form a strong bond upon exposure, making them ideal for applications where fast and precise adhesion is required. Non-UV Curable adhesives, on the other hand, cure without the need for UV light, enabling flexibility in terms of curing time and allowing for use in various assembly processes. Both adhesives cater to the needs of the camera and touch sensor assembly industry, providing reliable bonding solutions.</p></p>
