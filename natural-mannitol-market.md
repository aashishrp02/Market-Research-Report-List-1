Natural mannitol is also used in pharmaceuticals as an excipient, predominantly in orally disintegrating tablets and inhalation formulations.</p><p>The natural mannitol market is witnessing significant growth due to increasing consumer demand for sugar-free and low-calorie food products. Growing health concerns, such as obesity and diabetes, have led consumers to seek healthier alternatives to sugar. This has propelled the demand for natural sweeteners like mannitol. Additionally, the rising demand for mannitol in the pharmaceutical industry for drug delivery systems and inhalation formulations is driving market growth.</p><p>The market analysis indicates that North America dominates the natural mannitol market, followed by Europe and the Asia Pacific.
