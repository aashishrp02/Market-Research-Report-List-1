<p><p>Earth Leakage Relays, also known as Earth Fault Relays or Residual Current Monitors, are protective devices used to detect small earth leakage currents in electrical systems. They are crucial for ensuring the safety of electrical installations by preventing electric shocks and fires caused by ground faults. Earth Leakage Relays continuously monitor the current flowing through the live conductor and the neutral conductor of an electrical circuit and trip the supply if any imbalance is detected.</p><p>The Earth Leakage Relays Market is expected to witness substantial growth in the coming years.
