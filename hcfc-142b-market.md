Developed countries have already ceased production, while developing countries are currently in the process of phasing out. This will likely lead to a decline in the demand for HCFC-142b in the long term.</p><p>However, in the short to medium term, there is expected to be a steady demand for HCFC-142b due to its widespread use in several industries, such as construction, automotive, and electronics. This demand is primarily driven by the growing construction sector and increasing demand for energy-efficient insulation materials.</p><p>Furthermore, the market for HCFC-142b is also likely to witness significant growth due to its use as a substitute for other blowing agents with higher environmental impacts, such as hydrofluorocarbons (HFCs).
