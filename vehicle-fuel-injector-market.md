<p><strong>The Vehicle Fuel Injector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gasoline Direct Injection (GDI)</li><li>Port Fuel Injectors (PFI)</li><li>Diesel Fuel Injectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicle Fuel Injector market consists of various types, including Gasoline Direct Injection (GDI), Port Fuel Injectors (PFI), and Diesel Fuel Injectors. GDI injectors deliver the fuel directly into the combustion chamber for gasoline engines, resulting in improved fuel efficiency and performance. PFI injectors spray fuel into the intake manifold before it reaches the combustion chamber, commonly used in older gasoline engines. Diesel Fuel Injectors, as the name suggests, inject fuel into the combustion chamber of diesel engines, enabling efficient combustion and higher power output.</p></p>
