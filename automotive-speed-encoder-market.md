<p><p>The Automotive Speed Encoder Market includes two major types: Axial Encoder and Radial Encoder. An Axial Encoder measures the speed and direction of rotation along an axial or linear path. It is commonly used in applications where linear movement is present, such as in fuel injection systems or throttle positioning. On the other hand, a Radial Encoder measures the speed and direction of rotation around a central axis. This type is widely used in automotive applications like wheel speed sensing or engine crankshaft positioning. Both types play crucial roles in determining and transmitting accurate speed-related information in automotive systems.</p></p>
