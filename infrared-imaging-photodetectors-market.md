<p><p>The infrared imaging photodetectors market is segmented into various types, including Gallium Nitride, Caesium Nitrate, Polyvinyl Fluorides, Cobalt Phthalocyanine, and Others. Cobalt Phthalocyanine offers high detection efficiency and is often used in high-performance applications.
