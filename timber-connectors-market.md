Three-way connectors are designed to connect three timber beams at a common point, forming a stable joint. Similarly, four-way connectors are used to join four timber elements at a central point, enabling the creation of complex structures. Apart from these specific types, there are other general timber connectors available that serve various purposes, like joist hangers, angle brackets, and post bases. These connectors contribute to the structural integrity and stability of timber constructions.</p></p>
