<p><p>The AT Torque Converter for Automobiles market consists of two types: Single-stage Torque Converter and Multistage Torque Converter. In a Single-stage Torque Converter, the converter contains a stator, impeller, and turbine arranged in a single stage. It offers more efficiency and simplicity in design. On the other hand, a Multistage Torque Converter has multiple stages consisting of multiple impellers and turbines, allowing for better torque multiplication and greater efficiency. Both types play a crucial role in efficiently transmitting power from the engine to the wheels in automatic transmissions.</p></p>
