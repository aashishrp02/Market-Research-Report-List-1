CHAdeMO is a DC Charging standard developed in Japan and commonly used by Asian car manufacturers. Tesla Supercharger is Tesla's exclusive DC charging network, compatible with their vehicles. GB/T is China's national standard for EV charging, primarily used in the domestic market. These different standards cater to specific regions and vehicle models.</p></p>
