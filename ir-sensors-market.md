<p><p>IR sensors are used to detect infrared radiation, which is present in all objects emitting heat. There are two main types of IR sensors: thermal type and quantum type. Thermal type sensors work by measuring the temperature difference between an object and its surroundings. They are commonly used in temperature measurement applications. On the other hand, quantum type sensors work by detecting photons emitted or absorbed by an object. These sensors are often used in applications such as night vision devices, surveillance systems, and remote controls.</p></p>
