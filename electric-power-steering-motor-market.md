<p><strong>The Electric Power Steering Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CEPS Motor</li><li>REPS Motor</li><li>PEPS Motor</li><li>EHPS Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Power Steering (EPS) motor market consists of several types: Column Electric Power Steering (CEPS) motor, Rack-assist Electric Power Steering (REPS) motor, Pinion-assist Electric Power Steering (PEPS) motor, and Electronic Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS) motor. CEPS motors are mounted on the steering column, REPS motors are integrated into the steering rack, PEPS motors are fitted on the pinion shaft, and EHPS motors combine electric and hydraulic technology. These variations provide different solutions for steering systems in vehicles, allowing for enhanced control and efficiency.</p></p>
