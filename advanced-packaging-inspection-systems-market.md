<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Advanced packaging inspection systems are used to inspect various packaging products for defects or errors. Optical-based packaging inspection systems utilize cameras and image analysis software to detect visual defects such as scratches, dents, or missing labels on packaging materials. On the other hand, infrared packaging inspection systems use infrared imaging technology to identify defects that may not be visible to the naked eye, such as leaks, weak seals, or foreign contaminants. Both types of systems play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and integrity of packaging materials and products.</p></p>
