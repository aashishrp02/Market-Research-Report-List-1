<p><p>Goat milk yogurt is available in different market types, including set-style and Swiss-style yogurt. Set-style yogurt refers to a gel-like consistency that is achieved by allowing the yogurt to set in the container. Swiss-style yogurt, on the other hand, is made by straining the yogurt after fermentation to create a thicker and smoother texture. Both of these market types offer variations in taste and texture, providing consumers with options when choosing goat milk yogurt.</p></p>
