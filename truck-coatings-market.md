<p><p>Truck coatings market includes different types of coatings used on trucks. Electrodeposition coating is a process where a thin layer of paint is applied to the truck's surface through an electric current. It provides excellent corrosion resistance. Primers are applied before the top coat to enhance adhesion and protect the surface. They also provide a smooth base for the top coat. Top coats are the final layer of paint applied to trucks, providing color and gloss while protecting against environmental factors like UV rays and chemical damage.</p></p>
