<p><p>The control cable market consists of three main types of cables: PUR, PVC, and TPE (thermoplastic elastomers). PUR cables are made of polyurethane and are known for their excellent durability, chemical resistance, and flexibility. PVC cables are made of polyvinyl chloride and are widely used due to their cost-effectiveness and good electrical properties. TPE cables are made of a blend of rubber and plastic, offering excellent flexibility, resistance to temperature variations, and mechanical strength. These different types cater to different applications, providing a range of options in the control cable market.</p></p>
