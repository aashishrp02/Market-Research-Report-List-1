<p><p>The Alpine Ski Bindings market consists of two main types: Frame Bindings and Tech Bindings. Frame Bindings are traditional bindings that attach the boot to the ski via a sturdy frame. They provide good stability and are suitable for skiers who prioritize security. On the other hand, Tech Bindings are lightweight bindings designed for backcountry skiing. They feature a pin system that connects the boot to the ski, allowing for efficient uphill climbing and improved freedom of movement. Tech Bindings are favored by experienced skiers seeking versatility and agility in challenging terrains.</p></p>
