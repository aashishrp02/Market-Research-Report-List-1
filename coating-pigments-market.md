<p><p>Coating pigments are used to enhance the color, texture, and durability of coatings. The market for coating pigments can be categorized into two main types: organic coating pigments and inorganic coating pigments. Organic pigments are derived from carbon-containing materials and provide vibrant and stable colors. They are often used in applications where colorfastness is important. On the other hand, inorganic pigments are made from mineral compounds and offer excellent chemical resistance and heat stability. They are commonly used in coatings that require high durability and weather resistance.</p></p>
