<p><p>Fiber Reinforced Polymer (FRP) rebars are composite materials made of a combination of fibers and a polymer matrix. FRP rebars find applications in various industries, including construction, highways and bridges, marine structures, and water treatment plants.</p><p>The market analysis of the FRP rebars industry indicates strong growth potential in the coming years. The global FRP rebars market was valued at USD 601 million in 2019 and is projected to reach USD 987 million by 2027, growing at a CAGR of 11.1% during the forecast period. The increasing demand for lightweight and durable construction materials, along with the need for corrosion-resistant reinforcement in harsh environmental conditions, is driving the market growth.</p><p>The construction industry is the primary driver for FRP rebars, with significant demand for infrastructure development and modernization. The use of FRP rebars in bridge rehabilitation and construction projects has gained traction due to their resistance to chemical exposure and long service life.
