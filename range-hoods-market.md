<p><p>A range hood, also known as a kitchen hood or exhaust hood, is a device that is installed above a cooktop or stove to remove airborne contaminants, odors, grease, and steam that are generated during cooking. As people are becoming more health-conscious and aware of indoor air pollution, the demand for range hoods is increasing. Additionally, the growing trend of open kitchen layouts and smart homes is also boosting the market growth. Technological advancements, such as the integration of sensor-based controls and energy-efficient features, are further contributing to the market expansion.</p><p>The future of the range hoods market looks even more promising. The increasing adoption of electric and induction cooktops is expected to drive the demand for range hoods as these cooktops lack natural ventilation. The rise in disposable income and changing lifestyles, particularly in developing countries, will also fuel market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the COVID-19 pandemic has heightened the importance of maintaining hygiene and cleanliness, leading to an increased demand for efficient ventilation systems like range hoods. With the ongoing focus on sustainability and energy efficiency, manufacturers are likely to introduce eco-friendly range hoods that offer improved air quality and reduced energy consumption.</p><p>Considering these factors, the range hoods market is projected to grow at a Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) of 14.6% during the forecasted period.
