<p><p>The Polystyrene Microsphere market can be classified into two types: Hollow Microsphere and Solid Microsphere. Hollow Microsphere refers to polystyrene particles with empty interiors, which are lightweight and offer benefits such as thermal insulation, low density, and reduced material usage. On the other hand, Solid Microsphere refers to polystyrene particles that are solid throughout. Solid Microspheres provide features like increased strength, improved durability, and can be easily modified for different applications. Despite their differences, both types of microspheres have various applications in industries such as construction, aerospace, automotive, and healthcare.</p></p>
