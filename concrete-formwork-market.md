<p><p>Concrete formwork market types include system column formwork, horizontal formwork, slipform, and vertical formwork. System column formwork involves the use of modular systems for constructing columns. Horizontal formwork is used for creating flat surfaces such as slabs and beams. Slipform method involves continuously pouring concrete into a moving formwork structure, allowing for continuous construction of walls and towers. Vertical formwork is utilized for creating upright structures like walls. These different types of formwork enable efficient and precise concrete construction, catering to various architectural and structural requirements.</p></p>
