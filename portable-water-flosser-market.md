<p><p>The Portable Water Flosser market consists of two types: Constant Frequency and Frequency Conversion. On the other hand, Frequency Conversion water flossers allow users to adjust the speed according to their preference or needs. This flexibility enables users to customize the water pressure for different areas of their mouth or varying levels of sensitivity. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and aim to provide efficient and personalized oral care solutions.</p></p>
