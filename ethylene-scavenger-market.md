<p><p>An ethylene scavenger is a type of substance or chemical compound that is employed to remove or reduce the levels of ethylene gas in various settings. However, when the production of ethylene gas surpasses the required levels, it can lead to premature spoilage and decay of perishable products. Ethylene scavengers are primarily used in industries such as agriculture, food and beverage, and logistics to prevent ethylene-induced damage and extend the shelf life of fresh produce.</p><p>The ethylene scavenger market is expected to witness robust growth in the coming years.
