<p><p>Semiconductor package substrates are used in PC markets to enhance the performance and functionality of integrated circuits. There are several types of substrates, including FC-BGA (flip chip ball grid array), FC-CSP (flip chip chip scale package), WB BGA (wire bond ball grid array), and WB CSP (wire bond chip scale package). FC-BGA consists of a flip chip mounted on a substrate with solder balls, while FC-CSP is a smaller version of FC-BGA. WB BGA and WB CSP use wire bonding to connect the chip and substrate. Each type offers unique features suitable for different PC applications.</p></p>
