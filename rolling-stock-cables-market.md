<p><p>Rolling Stock Cables are categorized into three types based on their wall thickness: Standard Wall, Medium Wall, and Thin Wall. Standard Wall cables have a thicker insulation layer, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications that require resistance against environmental factors. Medium Wall cables have a moderate insulation layer and are commonly used in medium-duty applications. Thin Wall cables have a thinner insulation layer, making them lightweight and flexible, ideal for low-duty applications where space is limited. These different types offer various levels of protection and flexibility to cater to different needs in the rolling stock industry.</p></p>
