<p><p>Semiconductor process control equipment market can be categorized into three types: metrology systems, inspection systems, and other market segments. Metrology systems refer to equipment used for measuring and analyzing various parameters during the semiconductor manufacturing process to ensure accuracy and quality control. Inspection systems, on the other hand, are used to detect defects, particles, or any other irregularities on the surface of the fabricated semiconductor wafers. Lastly, the "other" market segment covers various equipment not falling under metrology or inspection systems, such as lithography systems, deposition systems, and etching systems, among others.</p></p>
