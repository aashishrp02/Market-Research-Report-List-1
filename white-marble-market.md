<p><p>The white marble market consists of two main types: natural marble and artificial marble. Natural marble is a genuine stone, quarried from the earth, possessing unique textures and patterns created by nature. It is highly valued for its elegance, durability, and natural beauty. On the other hand, artificial marble is a man-made product created using a combination of natural marble chips, resins, and pigments. It imitates the appearance and properties of natural marble, offering a cost-effective and customizable alternative for various applications. Both natural and artificial marble cater to different market demands and preferences.</p></p>
