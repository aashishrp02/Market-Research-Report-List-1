<p><p>NB Latex, also known as nitrile butadiene latex, is a type of synthetic latex produced from the copolymerization of butadiene and acrylonitrile monomers. It possesses excellent properties such as high tensile strength, puncture resistance, chemical resistance, and abrasion resistance, making it highly suitable for various applications.</p><p>The NB Latex market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing demand from end-use industries such as healthcare, automotive, construction, and personal care is driving the market growth. In the healthcare industry, NB Latex is extensively used in manufacturing medical gloves, surgical gloves, and other protective equipment due to its superior barrier properties against viruses and bacteria. The growing awareness regarding safety and hygiene across various sectors is further fueling the market growth.</p><p>Another key factor contributing to the market growth is the rising concern for environmental sustainability. Unlike natural rubber latex, NB Latex is not derived from trees and is free from proteins that can cause allergies. This makes it a preferred choice for manufacturers and consumers, leading to increased adoption.</p><p>Moreover, the latest trend observed in the NB Latex market is the development of eco-friendly and bio-based alternatives.
