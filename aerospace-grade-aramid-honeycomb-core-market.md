<p><p>The aerospace-grade aramid honeycomb core market offers three types of core shapes: hexagonal, rectangular (over-expanded), and pentagonal honeycomb cores. Hexagonal honeycomb cores are structured with hexagonal cells arranged in a regular pattern, providing optimal mechanical strength and load-bearing capabilities. Rectangular (over-expanded) honeycomb cores are manufactured using an over-expanded process, resulting in rectangular cells that offer increased strength and stiffness. Pentagonal honeycomb cores, on the other hand, have a pentagonal cell structure that offers excellent energy absorption and impact resistance. These different core shapes cater to specific requirements and applications within the aerospace industry.</p></p>
