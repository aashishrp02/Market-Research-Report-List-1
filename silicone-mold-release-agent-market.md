<p><p>The silicone mold release agent market consists of two main types: spray and liquid. Spray release agents are applied in aerosol form, allowing for easy and even distribution of the product onto the mold surface. They are commonly used in industries where quick and convenient application is required. On the other hand, liquid release agents are applied directly onto the mold surface using various methods such as brush or cloth. They offer more control over the application process and are often preferred for specific mold designs or when a thicker coating is desired.</p></p>
