<p><p>The electronic material recycling market is divided into two main types: physical method and chemical method. The physical method involves the mechanical disassembly of electronic devices to separate different components and materials. It may include processes like shredding, crushing, and sieving. On the other hand, the chemical method relies on various chemical reactions to extract valuable materials from electronic waste. This may involve techniques such as leaching, electrolysis, and pyrolysis. Both methods aim to recover and reuse valuable resources from electronic waste while reducing environmental impact.</p></p>
