<p><p>The Camel Milk market can be categorized into two types: fresh camel milk and processed camel milk. Fresh camel milk refers to the milk that is obtained directly from camels and consumed without undergoing any treatment. It is known for its high nutritional value and is usually available in regions with a nomadic camel-rearing culture. On the other hand, processed camel milk undergoes treatments such as pasteurization, homogenization, or fermentation to prolong its shelf life and ensure safety. It is more commonly found in urban areas and packaged for commercial distribution.</p></p>
