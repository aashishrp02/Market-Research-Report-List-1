<p><p>The optical chip market can be classified into two main types: optical active chip and optical passive chip. Optical active chips are designed to generate, amplify, and control the flow of light signals in optical communication systems. They play a crucial role in transmitting and receiving information over long distances. On the other hand, optical passive chips are used for various functions, such as splitting, coupling, and filtering light signals, without requiring any external power source. Both types of chips are essential components in the development of high-speed and efficient optical communication networks.</p></p>
