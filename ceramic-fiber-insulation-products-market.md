<p><p>Ceramic Fiber Insulation Products are available in different forms, including Ceramic Fiber Blanket and Ceramic Fiber Board. Ceramic Fiber Blanket is a flexible and lightweight insulation product made from ceramic fibers, offering excellent thermal insulation. It is widely used in various industries for applications such as furnace lining, kiln insulation, and pipe insulation. On the other hand, Ceramic Fiber Board is a rigid panel made from ceramic fibers, providing high-temperature resistance and low thermal conductivity. It is commonly used for furnace and boiler insulation, as well as walls and ceilings in industrial buildings. Both products contribute to energy efficiency and temperature regulation in various settings.</p></p>
