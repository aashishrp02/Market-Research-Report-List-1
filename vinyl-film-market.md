<p><p>The vinyl film market can be categorized into two types: clear and opaque. Clear vinyl film refers to a transparent film that allows light to pass through it, providing visibility to objects underneath. It is commonly used for protective purposes, such as covering documents or surfaces. Opaque vinyl film, on the other hand, is non-transparent and prevents light from passing through. It is often used for decorative purposes, providing privacy or adding color to windows, walls, or other surfaces. Both types serve different functions based on their transparency properties.</p></p>
