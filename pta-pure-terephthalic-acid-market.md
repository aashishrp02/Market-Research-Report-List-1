<p><p>PTA (Pure Terephthalic Acid) is a chemical compound primarily used in the production of polyester. The market for PTA is dominated by different process types. The Amoco Process involves the oxidation of p-xylene to produce PTA with high purity levels. Cooxidation is a process where p-xylene is oxidized using oxygen and acetic acid in the presence of a catalyst. Multistage Oxidation is a method that utilizes various reactors to gradually convert p-xylene into PTA. The Henkel Process involves the oxidation of a mixture of para-xylene and ortho-xylene to produce PTA.</p></p>
