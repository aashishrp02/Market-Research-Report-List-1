The market is primarily driven by the increasing adoption of diesel engines in automobiles, especially in developing countries. Diesel engines are known for their higher fuel efficiency and lower carbon dioxide emissions compared to gasoline engines. However, they also produce higher NOx emissions, which has led to the adoption of NSR catalysts.</p><p>Furthermore, governments globally have implemented strict regulations to curb vehicular emissions. For instance, the Euro 6 emission standards in Europe have set a limit on NOx emissions for vehicles. Such regulations have created a lucrative market for NSR catalysts, as they help automakers to comply with the emission standards.</p><p>Latest Trends:</p><p>One of the major trends in the Reduction (NSR) Catalyst Market is the development of advanced catalyst materials to improve their efficiency.
