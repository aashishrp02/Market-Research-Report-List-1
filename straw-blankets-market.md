<p><p>Straw blankets, used for erosion control and slope stabilization, are available in two main types: single net and double net straw blankets. Single net straw blankets feature a single layer of netting, typically made of biodegradable material, to hold the straw together. These blankets are suitable for areas with gentle slopes and low erosion risks. On the other hand, double net straw blankets have two layers of netting, providing enhanced strength and durability. They are recommended for areas with steeper slopes and higher erosion potential. Both types serve the purpose of preventing soil erosion and promoting vegetation growth.</p></p>
