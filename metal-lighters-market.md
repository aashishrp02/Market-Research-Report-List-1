<p><p>Metal lighters are available in two main types: Flint Type and Electronic Type. Flint Type lighters utilize a flint wheel and wick system, where the flint wheel creates a spark that ignites the fuel-soaked wick, producing a flame. These lighters require regular flint replacements and refilling of fuel. On the other hand, Electronic Type lighters are powered by a rechargeable battery, which generates an electric spark to ignite the fuel. These lighters do not require fuel refills but need recharging periodically. Both types offer portable and convenient fire-starting solutions for various applications.</p></p>
