<p><ul><li>Weldable Clip</li><li>Boltable Clip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rail clips are an essential component of railway systems. Weldable clips are designed to be permanently welded onto the base of the rail, providing a reliable connection. They offer excellent durability and resistance, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Boltable clips, on the other hand, are secured to the rail base by bolts. This type of clip allows for easy installation and removal, making it more convenient for maintenance and repairs. Both types of rail clips play a crucial role in ensuring the stability and safety of railway tracks.</p></p>
