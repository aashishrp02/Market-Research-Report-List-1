<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The underwater yacht light market consists of two types: through-hull and surface-mount lights. Through-hull lights are installed by drilling a hole in the hull of the yacht and inserting the light fixture. They offer a seamless and flush appearance and are typically used in larger yachts. On the other hand, surface-mount lights are attached to the exterior of the yacht's hull. They are easier to install as no drilling is required, making them more popular for smaller yachts. Both types provide beautiful underwater illumination for yachts, enhancing their aesthetics and visibility.</p></p>
