<p><p>Hardwood flooring market comprises three major types: real-wood floor, engineered floor, and laminated floor. Real-wood floor is made from a solid piece of wood and is known for its durability and authenticity. Engineered floor is constructed using multiple layers, with a top layer of real wood and lower layers made of plywood or fiberboard, resulting in a stable and less expensive alternative. Laminated floor, on the other hand, is a synthetic product that mimics the appearance of real wood using a photographic layer placed on top of a compressed fiberboard.</p></p>
